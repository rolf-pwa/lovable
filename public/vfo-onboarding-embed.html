VFO Onboarding — Iframe Embed Test

This page demonstrates how the VFO Onboarding widget will appear inside an iframe on your site (e.g., Wix).

Copy this iframe code into your Wix site:

<iframe src="https://app.prosperwise.ca/vfo-onboarding/embed" width="100%" height="700" style="border: none; border-radius: 16px;" title="ProsperWise VFO Onboarding" allow="clipboard-write"> </iframe>
